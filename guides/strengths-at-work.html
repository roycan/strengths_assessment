Using Your Strengths at Work

Practical ways to turn insight into impact.

Intro: Your signature strengths are where energy meets performance. Here are practical, low-lift ways to apply them in your work week.

Start with What’s Strong

Pick one top strength from your results and design a 15-minute experiment around it today.

Design Small Experiments

  • Curiosity: Ask two open questions in your next meeting.
  • Hope: Start the meeting by naming one positive outcome you’re aiming for.
  • Perseverance: Timebox one important task and finish it fully.

Reflect and Iterate

What changed? What felt energizing? Repeat what works, adjust what doesn’t.
